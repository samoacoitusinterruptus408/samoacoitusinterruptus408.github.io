## 💡 Best Practices for Data Recovery

Data recovery is a delicate process. Follow these tips to ensure success:

*   **Stop Writing Data**: Once you realize data is missing, stop saving new files to the affected RAID array. New data overwrites deleted data.
*   **Use External Storage**: Always recover your files to an external hard drive or a different physical disk attached to your computer.
*   **Check Hardware**: If your RAID array failed because of a physical disk crash, replacing the damaged disk is the first step. The software helps you reconstruct the array configuration once you verify the physical hardware.
*   **Verify Permissions**: Ensure your user account has administrative privileges. The software needs low-level access to the disk hardware.
